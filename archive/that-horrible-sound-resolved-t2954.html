Hey that sound isn't so bad is it? At least you know your sound card is working __{{emoticon}}__

Ok to get rid of it, in /home/username/.fluxbox/startup /home/username/.icewm/startup files, (found in antiXcc) just comment out the cacophony __{{emoticon}}__ like this:

#play /usr/share/sounds/Pardus_Rocks/Pardus_Rocks-Logout.ogg > /dev/null 2>&1 &
