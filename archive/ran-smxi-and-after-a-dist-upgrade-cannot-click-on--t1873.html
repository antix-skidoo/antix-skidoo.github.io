Sounds like something got uninstalled in XFCE when you upgraded. I did a BOO BOO kinda like that once in AntiX 8.0. My mistake was hitting the upgrade button in Synaptic, After it was done and I rebooted, I lost synaptic package manager because of some Gnome installs on upgrade. To fix it, I did a, apt-get install synaptic, and when it showed what was going to be installed and what needed to be removed with ,apt-get autoclean, I saw what had killed synaptic and it was all gnome related.

You might consider doing a , apt-get --purge remove xfce, then a , apt-get autoremove , then reinstall xfce with a , apt-get update, then apt-get install xfce. Instead of reinstalling 8.2 Final again.
