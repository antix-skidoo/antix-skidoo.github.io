You graphics are falling back to software rendering in OpenGL, which is why your CounterStrike frame rates stink.

Broadwell based chips can be problematic. you could try to update the xserver-xorg-video-intel package to the one in jessie-backports.

You could also try the 64 bit antiX-16 beta 5, which I believe already has the updated driver, and see if it works better for you.
