look in hidden folder .icewm for toolbar text file. Open it up.

Code: Select all

prog"Leafpad""/usr/share/pixmaps/leafpad.png" leafpad
Put that line where you wish to see it in the toolbar. Next to terminal or browser or what ever.
Save it.
Restart Icewm with Menu>logout>restart icewm.
