Welcome to antix,

here's one way, you may want a different icon but you get the idea, open control center> edit icewm settings>toolbar and add the following:

prog"Mail" /usr/share/iceape/chrome/icons/default/msgcomposeWindow.png iceape -mail

Then click on anXti (Favorite Applications) or menu(right click on desktop) all the same, go to logout and click Restart Icewm
