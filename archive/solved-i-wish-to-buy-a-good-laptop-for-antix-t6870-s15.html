Possibly useful information:

For one laptop with Intel HD 5500 graphics, I had to install the Antix 4.10 kernel and the jessie-backports version of xserver-xorg-video-intel v2.99. In fact, I had to remaster a USB stick to do so, since it would become unstable before I could update with the stock 4.4 kernel.

For another laptop with Intel HD 520 graphics, the 4.4 kernel and stock jessie video seems to work fine.
