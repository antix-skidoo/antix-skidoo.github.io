In earlier replies, I neglected to point out that rox-filer keybinds (accelerators) are too-easily set (or accidentally changed)
by pressing a key or a keycombo while mouse cursor is hovering over the opened rox-filer contextmenu.

Possibly, Rademes had accidentally pressed [Ctrl+C] while hovering some other menu entry, causing that accelerator to be assigned to some other command.
