Royal Blue antiX has been popular and I really enjoy using it. I am a big fan of Richard Mohler's wallpaper"colorfast" the inspiration behind Royal Blue antiX. When my wife saw"colorfast" on my antiX machine I got a"wow" out of her...not easy to do. Mohler's abstract wallpapers have an energy and life of their own.

Richard created several wallpapers in the yellow-orange spectrum that I like so I whipped up another antiX style color variant....."Radiant antiX". I guess I'm slowly building an antiX style color pack. I designed Radiant antiX to work fairly well with either of Richard's wallpapers"orangyart" or"radiant".

I'm comfortable enough in my masculinity to admit I really love the color yellow. Yellow is bright and cheery....easily inducing a smile or brightening the mood.
