Desert four:
Ingredients - Can of whipped cream, peach, safety glasses
Instructions - Put on safety glasses. Blast a small (or large) amount of whipped cream on peach. Take a bite. Repeat.

Desert five:
Ingredients - package of cream cheese (softened), package of oreo cookies, package of chocolate chips.
Instructions - mix cream cheese and oreos in a food processor. form into small balls about the size of cherry tomatoes. melt the chocolate chips in a double boiler. dip the balls into the chocolate and place on a cookie sheet lined with waxed paper. put in the fridge until cool.
