I just did a quick test on antiX-16-full 32 bit (coming soon) in Virtualbox.

icewm - 47.9 MB RAM (default background image)
fluxbox - 47.5 MB RAM (default background image)
jwm - 46.5MB RAM (default background image)
openbox - 65.3MB RAM (no background image) - 54.8MB with wicd disabled.
blackbox - 43.3MB RAM (no background image)
jwm - no background image (ie just like openbox) - 35.9MB)
herbstluftwm - 51,5MB RAM

Clear winner in terms of RAM = jwm
Clear loser in terms of RAM = openbox

Added:

icewm - no background image (like blackbox and openbox) - 37.3MB RAM
fluxbox - no background image (like blackbox and openbox) - 37.7MB RAM
