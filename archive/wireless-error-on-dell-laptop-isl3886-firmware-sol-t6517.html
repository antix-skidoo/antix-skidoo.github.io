Well, I tried a 4th time starting from a fresh download and this time it worked. The only change was the fresh download.

blacklist prism54
modprobe -r prism54
modprobe -r p54pci
download file from site above, rename to isl3886pci, copy to /lib/firmware and also /usr/local/firmware
modprobe p54pci
iwconfig
ceni

thanks, your suggestion worked
