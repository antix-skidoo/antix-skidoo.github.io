antiX's SLiM is configured to use ~/.xinitrc for the login. This means, I think, that if you haven't installed the default /home dir then it won't work.
So you may boot the liveCD again and copy its /home contents to your installed /home. Just pay attention if it will overwrite some app configuration that you may want to keep (both PCManFM and ROX-Filer will ask you beforehand).
