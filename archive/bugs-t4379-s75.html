Off Topic
dolphin_oracle wrote:...it was dumb luck
The list of coincidences to get to this position is long.
  • A few years ago I create a theme for IceWM that includes a definition for the tooltip
  • A new version of antiX is released that has Zukitwo as the default theme
  • Zukitwo includes a definition for the tooltip
  • Both tooltip definitions produce very simliar results
  • The similarity is sufficently close to allow the fact that there are two definitions actively in play to go unnoticed.
  • The combination remains in use until 13.2 is released
  • 13.2 drops Zukitwo and Mediterranean replaces it as the default theme
  • Mediterranean includes a definition for the tooltip
  • The difference between the Mediterranean and IceWM tooltips are sufficiently different to expose the fact that there are two definitions actively in play
It makes me smile and wonder why we direct our time and energy towards computers. Then I look back at the improvements in antiX over a very few years and see the answer.


On Topic
dolphin_oracle wrote:lxappearance does not appear able to override the colors of the themes.
I also find this. Changes are not saved to the configuration files.
dolphin_oracle wrote:...I had to edit /usr/share/themes/"theme name"/gtkrc manually to change the tooltip colors.
I find two locations need to be edited with the tooltip definition. Using the MediterraneanWhite theme in antiX-13.1-Full-32bit-Stable and antiX-13.2-Full-64bit-Stable:
  1. /usr/share/themes/THEMENAME/gtk-2.0/gtkrc
  2. /usr/share/themes/THEMENAME/gtk-3.0/settings.ini
This appears to provide consistency in the tooltip presentation in the following test examples
  • Items in IceWM taskbar and tray
  • ROX-Filer (responds to changes in .../gtk-2.0/gtkrc but not .../gtk-3.0/settings.ini)
  • File-Roller (responds to changes in .../gtk-3.0/settings.ini but not .../gtk-2.0/gtkrc)

Unable to mount NFS Shares in antiX-13.2

Connectshares is designed to mount NFS and Samba shares. Mounting Samba & Windows shares is working OK. Mounting of NFS shares fails.

Removal of nfs-common in 13.2 is the cause. Reinstating this as a dependency will return Connectshares to a fully working condition straight-out-of-the-box.
