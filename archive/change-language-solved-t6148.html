Welcome. If you just want to toggle the keyboard from English to Greek (like I do), then edit etc/default/keyboard to look something like this

Code: Select all

XKBMODEL="pc105"
XKBLAYOUT="us,gr"
XKBVARIANT=","
XKBOPTIONS="grp:alt_shift_toggle,terminate:ctrl_alt_bksp,grp_led:scroll"
BACKSPACE="guess"
