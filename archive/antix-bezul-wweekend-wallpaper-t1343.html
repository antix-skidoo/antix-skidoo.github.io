Thanks, sak. I found the tyrex icewm theme at freshmeat. The theme only had images for the windows - no taskbar, etc. Some months later, someone wrote a clearlooks theme for icewm and uploaded it at box-look. Out of all the taskbars I tried, the clearlooks one paired the best with tyrex. So I blended the two together, added some icons from the truecurve icewm theme, and recolored the menu highlight bar. I uploaded the theme to anti. It may be on the next iso.

Again, thanks and glad you like it. I'm never sure how ports of other themes will go over. Some people enjoy having a familiar theme, and some want a unique theme. At any rate, I think the colors of tyrex go very well with your outstanding wall.
