Just to tell you that in a stock antiX you can resize an image with mtPaint. If it is already in the right aspect ratio then you use Image > Scale Canvas.... If you need to crop it to fit then you select what you want and press Ctrl+Shift+X to crop. Then Save or Save as...
Of course, if it has the same aspect ratio there's no need to resize or scale: you can just use the 'Scaled' option in Nitrogen (the background chooser).
