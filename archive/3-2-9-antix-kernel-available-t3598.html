Firstly, antiX kernel is 486 so k5/k6 and PI will boot, unlike aptosid, siduction etc.
It also has more more built in kernel options for live use. (rather than modules).

It is a mixture of Debian, siduction and aptosid kernels in terms of patches. I didn't use all of their patches. So I guess, antiX kernel is more 'vanilla'
