I know rox desktop lets your icons be moved anywhere you want so maybe switch?

Right click any desktop icon and choose ROX-Filer>Options>Pinboard>Pinboard behaviour and choose an appropriate grid step for the icon size you are using. The icons will automagically fall into line with the chosen grid pattern when you move them around manually.

There is no alignment tool per se, but the invisible grid makes it easier than doing it by eye. The coarser the grid step the easier the alignment.
