Just an FYI.

Audio/Video worked with no problems with streamlight, vlc etc..., however, audio was not working with html5 or webm videos on sites like yahoo.com or youtube. Had to install pulseaudio (thankfully it came from nosystemd repo) and now audio is working on all sites. This was a full antiX 15 install on an HP AMD Athlon II with Radeon 64bit etc...

Wanted to post in case anyone else had a similar problem with internet audio not working.


Update: While the above tip did work, I realized upon installing antix15 on a laptop (where sound worked without problems) that it was a configuration problem. Copied all .config & ."files" from liveCD to /home and all is good! Removed pulseaudio and pavucontrol. __{{emoticon}}__
