I won a Kindle Fire at work. Not something I would ever buy... Anyway, with a new KF you get 1 month free membership to Amazon Prime. Amazon Prime also has streaming videos. For me, HTML5 seemed to use about 10-15% less CPU power, when compared to Flash. The quality between the two was imperceptible to me.
