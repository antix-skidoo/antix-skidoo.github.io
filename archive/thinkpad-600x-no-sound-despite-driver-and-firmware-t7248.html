Dear all,

I thought everything was fine since the mixer showed the card correctly etc - but once I really wanted to listen to radio I realized:
- Thinkpad 600x (full extension of CPU and RAM) with antiX 16.2 does not play sound.
- Driver is snd-cs46xx. This requires an extra firmware package which I installed from also-1.0.28-firmware.
- dmesg only points out, that the crystal sound card shares IRQ 11 with Cardbus and mini-PCI-WLAN-card. No complaints (any more) about driver or firmware not loading or the (in)famous FIFO waiting fault.
--> short: driver seems to load and work, no messages.
- check sound from control center also says it is able to contact the card - only I hear nothing.
- alsamixer: all channels are unmuted, card is correctly seen (it seems) - but no sound.
- in DOS, OS/2, WinXp (Multiboot setup) soundplays fine.

--> where else can I search for the fault???

Torsten

BTW: other than the sound issue - the old TP600x really works fine. 80MB of 570MB are used o n idle desktop or when running just a few terminals, no swap used at all, most of the time around 10% CPU load - and the GUI reacts fast.
Firefox sometimes gets a bit slow, but that happens even on a dual core every now and then...
