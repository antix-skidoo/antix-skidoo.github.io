    When Mepis 7 goes final, I will make available an upgraded version of antiX Lysistrata (antiX-M7.01).
    The main reason is because there have been quite a few changes in Mepis 7 since antiX Lysistrata went final. For example in the kernel, Mepis utilities. (as well as changes in Debian of course)
    I noticed that upgrading a freshly installed antiX brings in 103MB of apps! Not good for someone on dial-up! Also not particularly good for
    new users of antiX who want to keep it up-to-date.

    I will also fix some bugs that were found, and hopefully ensure that upgrading/installing will be easier/less problematic than at present.

    So, if you can remember, post here any bugs that need to be fixed.
