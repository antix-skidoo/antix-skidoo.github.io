To install. Just download the zip or tar to Downloads folder. Open rox and just left click which opens Xarchiver.
Pick extract. You should end up with a folder or a .wxz. Open another rox window. Hit the eye to see hidden files. Go to .xmms folder. Open it up. Open the Skins folder. Drag and drop the extracted folder or .wxz from Downloads open rox window to Skins open rox window and select copy or move (depending on whether you plan on making a backup of downloaded skins).

Open XMMS or QMMP. Left click on top left hand corner of player and pick Options>Skin Browser.
Pick your new skin, (left click on names in left window), see your new skin show up and then hit close.
