I'd just like to repeat what anti said, so you know it works the same for someone else. After lots of trial and error, I found that it's best to mount and unmount any usb (hd or stick) with pcmanfm. I usually just use copy and paste with pcmanfm now, but rox also works after the hd or stick has been mounted. In wbar and the menu, I set it so the /media folder opens with pcmanfm, but the /home folder opens with rox (I think antiX does this, but I had edited my menu otherwise earlier.) This way I have access to either rox or pcmanfm very quickly.
