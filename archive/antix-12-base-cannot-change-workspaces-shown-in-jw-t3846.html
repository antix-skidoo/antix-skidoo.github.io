In the installed version of JWM, the method of changing this is deprecated (non working).
The fix is to change the entry in ~/.jwm/preferences
Remove

Code: Select all

<Desktops count="4">
Replace with

Code: Select all

<Desktops height="2" width="2">
This will produce 4 small workspaces in a 2x2 square block and reduce the area required in the panel, or other combination the user prefers.
