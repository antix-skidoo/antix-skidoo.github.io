arinbasu,

There are a couple of things you might try, I resolved my hibernation woes by installing uswsusp and it's depends, I used synaptic for this chore.
Now when I close my laptop and reopen I get a black screen,
I then hit ctrl-alt-F1 then hit ctrl-alt-F7 and everything is back to normal.

Also Dave resolved his issue by installing acpi-support.
Also I have in my menu.lst
resume= /dev/hda2

this points to my swap partition, your's will be different.

There are so many variables that is really a matter of trial and error.
