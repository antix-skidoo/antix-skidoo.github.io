Here are some themes with a flat look to them. In fact, the window title bars and taskbar remind me more of fluxbox than icewm. I found LightBox at kde-look and it became one my favorite themes. I added some mail and other icons from the theme sky to complete the theme and named it SkyBox. I think I found dotFLAT at kde-look as well. It had no icons or taskbar definition, so I blended the taskbar from LightBox and the icons from SkyBox. I named the result FlatBoxLight. Finally, I can't remember where I found mavvert, but it's a nice theme. The title bar reminds me of openbox titlebars. Mavvert uses the default icewm taskbar, but does have some custom colors. It's kinda dark with purple accents for the active bits of the taskbar. I thought that the color scheme was similar to the shicewm themes (over at box-look) and so blended a purple shicewm taskbar and some icons from Sky with Mavvert. The result overall is like a dark version of LightBox. I call this blending shiceMavvert. I hope you enjoy.
