i am assuming you are using icewm.

from your other posts, I see you may be using fluxbox. To get the list by workspace that you are after, do a middle click on the desktop. this will bring up the workspaces menu. if you scroll through the workspaces, you will see a list of all open apps by workspace.

you can set a key combination for this if you don't have a middle mouse button. the keys file is what you are after, located in ~/.fluxbox
