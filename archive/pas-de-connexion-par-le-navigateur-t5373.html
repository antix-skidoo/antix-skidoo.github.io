Which antiX did you install? AntiX 13.1 or 13.2? In either, you need to go to the Control Center--> Network--> wcid --> then to the arrow to the upper Right to set your Preferences. Since you are using wi-fi, you may have to set the wiireless to wlan0 (or maybe wlan1). Then you need to REFRESH. Your wifi should appear, and you may have to enter your password. You should be able to connect after that.
