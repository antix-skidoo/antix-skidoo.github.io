skeeter,

I use

wodim --devices

to determine correct drive, then

wodim -sao speed=4 any.iso

(any.iso= whatever iso you want to burn)

then to check the md5sum

md5sum /dev/cdrom

with the cdrom being the correct drive from wodim --devices
