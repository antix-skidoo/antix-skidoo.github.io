My laptop locks up with a lot of distros. Sometimes using the cheatcode"noscsi" works well if your drive is sata. Use the extra options option when grub loads and append the cheatcode, without quotes, at the end. (This worked on the last distro I previewed, but I still ran into an error while starting avahi near the end of the boot process. That I haven't figured out yet.) Anyway, good luck.
