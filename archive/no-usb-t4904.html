try either using Menu>Tools>Filers>spacefm to mount your usb sticks, or use the mount tool on the disks tab in the control center.

If you use the spacefm desktop, mounting will be automatic. there are a host of other options for this.

you can test your camera with guvcview found in the Menu>Applications>Sound and video>guvcview.
