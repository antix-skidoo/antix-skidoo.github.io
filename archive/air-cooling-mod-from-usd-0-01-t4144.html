    The hole at the place of the missing case fan closed and PCI slot below AGP card opened. So intake air goes directly to VGA & chipset heatsink.
    External air flow flap applied to direct outgoing air upwards, so it doesn't sinked again.

    Result: CPU core idle temperature lowered by 8°C, measured at 21°C ground level room temperature.
    Price of materials used: $ 0.01. ( 1x used A4 paper, 1x used A4 cover, 10 cm adhesive tape). Worktime: 30 minutes.
