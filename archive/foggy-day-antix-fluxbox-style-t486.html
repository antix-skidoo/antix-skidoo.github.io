Foggy Day is a departure for me because of the utilization of lighter colors. I discovered using a black & white wallpaper presents a lot of challenges in finding the right shades for the text colors to aid in visibility. I wanted something quiet and understated but with a few splashes of color here and there to delight the eye. I'm still not sure about the solid orange in the conky graphs...might try changing that a little to ease off the orange even more. Anyway....another good learning experience.

Other Vermaden styles inspired my style choices including"solaris" and"aquabox". Those excellent styles work with this wallpaper and others but will not look right"out of the box" with antiX. A quick change of the text parameters in the style config fixes that, however. You can go to box-look.org to download those styles.
