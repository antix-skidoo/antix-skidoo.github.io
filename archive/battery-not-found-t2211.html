You may just have the wrong battery listed in conky. If you have BAT0 listed try changing it to BAT1. Mine is seen BAT1.

Code: Select all

${color white}${execi 10 (acpi -b | awk '{print $3, $4}')} ${color white}${battery_bar BAT1}
