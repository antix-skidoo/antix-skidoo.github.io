I have been checking with smxi to see what warnings h2 has had and what is going to be removed/installed. Today it allowed xfce 4.6 to be installed, xorg got upgraded plus some miscellaneous app changes. I also installed the latest sidux kernel.

Well, although the system is working, this is what I've found:

1-as warned, fglrx won't work with the new xorg. changed to radeon driver--no problem

2-lost scroll and tap on the touchpad-PITA

3-usb sticks will not mount automatically. Thunar normally would mount and open these without issue now it just throws errors. I must mount manually.

4-because the xfce4.6 packages are not complete, missing xfce-session, I cannot shut down normally, it just takes me to the slim log in screen.

5-xfce menus are kind of scattered, probably due to missing packages.

I'm sure there's more to pop up and will let you know.

Conclusions: Unless you have an extremely good reason to upgrade, DON"T. Wait for everything to settle down. I'm sure it won't take long until everything is back to normal.
