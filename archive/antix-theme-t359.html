Well here is one attempt John. Not sure exactly what you were looking for, but the antix logo is a blue globe so I thought I would play off of that.

I took a NASA night photo of the earth, made the oceans mostly transparent, mapped it to a globe, and turned the globe so Greece (Anticapitalista) was centralized. Then placed a little star over Anti's home and added the text. I cloned out the previous logo and added the globe.

I could add a little highlighting because the globe doesn't reflect the fact light is shining on it. I thought I would see what you thought of it before I did much more. I'm playing with a real color globe at the moment and will probably post that later.

You should make sure this will not violate any copyright laws with the existing wallpaper before you publish it. I cloned out the previous author's name in the corner. Under magnification it looked like this had been hacked from something before but it wouldn't hurt to check.

Edit: Forgot to mention I went this way first because the style you had in the screenshot looked dark blue. Not sure if this is real close or not.
