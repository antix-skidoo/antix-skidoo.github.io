Hello Krane:

Welcome to Antix! The menus (either for IceWM or Fluxbox) are fully customizable in Antix. Just use the control center>Desktop>Edit Fluxbox Setttings (or IceWm). This will bring up a window with several files. Click on menu and follow the syntax to add programs to the menu or to rearrange the categories.

For your second question, I typically use apt-get as root from a terminal. I suppose Synaptic will work as well. Since I use apt-get to update and upgrade, it's just easier for me to do apt-get remove or apt-get purge.

Enjoy Antix and post away any questions you may have, and some one will try to help.
