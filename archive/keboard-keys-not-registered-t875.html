Here is my new Xorg.conf entry for the keyboard :

Section"InputDevice"
Identifier"Keyboard0"
Driver"keyboard"
Option"CoreKeyboard"
Option"XkbModel""pc102"
Option"XkbLayout""ch"
Option"XKbVariant""fr" # French
EndSection
