It sounds like you have your rox pinboard enabled. If you do, you should see your home folder in the top-left corner. Left-click on the folder. Right-click in a white space. Click options. Then click types. Near the bottom is a drop-down menu you can use to choose an icon theme.

If you want additional icon themes, download and then extract them (as root) to /usr/share/icons.
