    You can add as many as you want. Some of the more popular desktops available are fluxbox, icewm, xfce, lxde, fvwm crystal, gnome, openbox, and kde. The basic steps are:

    1. Install from the repos
    2. Add an entry in /etc/slim.conf
    3. Add an entry in ~/.xinitrc

    Have fun.
