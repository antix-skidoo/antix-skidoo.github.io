Yeah, we'll let the 'kids' give us the instructions for irc.
I'm a relative n00b at this too.

antiX comes with irssi (CLI, but very cool!) and xchat (GUI and also the coolest of the GUI chat apps, so I have been told) for this thing.

This is what I do in irssi. NB I'm nowher near an expert at this.

1. Open irssi from the menu
2. Type /connect irc.freenode.net or /connect funky.zoite.net then enter
3. Type /join #antiX (for freenode) or /join #antix (for zoite)
4. To join in the chat, just type your message.

That is the basics as I see it.
Let's hope the irc gurus can help us old fogies out here!
