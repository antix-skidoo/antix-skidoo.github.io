Here's my first attempt at a custom theme for IceWM.
It's various shades of brown , fairly subdued , easy on the eyes.
Any and all comments are OK.
I don't know how to offer it up as a download , if you PM me , I can email it to you.
I think it's about 88Kb , not too large anyway.



Unlike the JWM themes , this is a folder with .xpm files and one text file.
I took a simple theme , recolored all the .xpm files , and rewrote the description text in a more readable fashion.
I used all other icon theme stuff as installed in version 15 , so none of that is affected by the theme.
I'm older , so the fonts are all in bold , it's easily changed to taste in the"default.theme" text file.
