Táncsics Mihály was a journalist, who was arrested because of his way of thinking. Táncsics was freed from the Habsburgs's prison on the 15th of March 1848, the very day when the Hungarian revolution broke out.

Now the USA, Israel, Germany, and France teamed up and requested the hungarian governement to take back the price from Mr.Szaniszló, because of his way of thinking. He also stated, that banks like FED & IMF are teaming up to take over Earth.
The USA announced, that they plan to discuss the topic at the UN congress, and start counter-measures against Hungary.
So the hungarian governement asked Mr. Szaniszló to give back the prize.

Mr. Szaniszló announced, that in a true democracy, and in a really independent state it would not happen, and it actually proves his statements, but gave back the prize to save our country.
