as requested by anticapitalista here's my how-to connect to wireless with ceni.
i'm posting it so you can easily read it here and i'm uploading it as a .txt file too:
this is a how-to for connecting to a wireless network using ceni:
-first open ceni, there are three ways to do this:
-open the control centre, go to the network tab and click on ceni.
-ope up your menu, go to applications, internet and then click on ceni.
-open your terminal,type su, enter your root password, press enter, type ceni, press enter.
-once ceni is open, select your wireless device either by
-clicking on it.
-or by selecting it with your keyboard arrows and then clicking enter.
-then click on scan, it will search for available networks.
-then select your network, if it hasn't found it, click scan again.
-then select your encryption and enter it.
-click accept.
-in the next step, it will be most likely you will just have to click accept again, so do this.
-it will now configure your network connection.
-once it's done, just press enter.
-then press enter again to exit.
and that's it! you should now have a working wireless connection!
have fun browsing the net!

there you have it, i hope it helped...
