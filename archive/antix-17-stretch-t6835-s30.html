actually, there are a couple of different ways to do this on the spacefm desktop. None are as easy as teh default rox desktop, but they do work. Remember, in spacefm, the desktop is a folder (in your home folder actually) and in rox, its just a link board.

1. right click on an existing desktop item, and choose New and then the item you want.

2. from within the spacefm filemanager, choose the item you want to link, right click on it, choose New then Link. Give the link a name and location.

3. If you just want to put apps on the desktop, drag and drop from /usr/share/applications, copying the appropriate application entry. You can also do apps from the from the right click of existing spacefm desktop items, choosing New then Desktop Applications.

SpaceFM actually provides three ways of drag&drop:
(1) Simple drag&drop will move a file.
(2) If you want to copy instead just press and hold down the the CTRL key while dragging (but let it go before dropping).
(3) CTRL+SHIFT while dragging creates a symlink.
Methods (2) and (3) work best when you start pressing the key(s) before grabbing the file.

This holds for drag&drop to/from the desktop as well as between any other folders (in different tabs/panels/windows of SpaceFM) – but doesn't seem to work between devices where you always end up in copying.

If desired the default drag behaviour can be changed: View -> Preferences -> Interface ->"Default Drag Action".

Just to let you know the present situation.
