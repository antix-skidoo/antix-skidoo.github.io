I set up my"windows" key to bring up the program menu in fluxbox. works when I have an app maximized that way. and the menu pops up wherever my eyes are. to be fair, I do he same thing in icewm too.

the fluxbox menu is also keyboard aware, in that you can type to the first few letters of an apps name. icewm is limited to the first character.

I like the smaller toolbar and the fact that fluxbox remembers my window positions/sizes better than icewm. I don't use a toolbar type panel, but rather I customize the fluxbox menu with my favorite apps on the top level of the menu. the fluxbox panel/toolbar does have a tasklist, workspace switcher, and system tray & clock by default.

in general, i think the fluxbox themes as a whole are nicer, but there are serveral really good icewm themes also included in antix.

other than that, force of habit.
