here it works, but the app doesn't receive focus until you let go of the keys. you can watch the selected app change in the toolbar. I have no idea if that is standard jwm behavior or not. seems weird though.

edit:

if you change this code:

Code: Select all

<Key mask="A" key="Tab">nextstacked</Key>
to

Code: Select all

<Key mask="A" key="Tab">next</Key>
then pressing alt+tab will cycle windows without having to let go of the alt key. each press of alt-tab will bring up the next window in cycle.
