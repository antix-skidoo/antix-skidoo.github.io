After a long time I come back to antix and installed M8.5 on a low-performace box Dell Dimension 3100.

After the installation there was no sound there. I need some times to discover, that the alsa-utils were not installed.
So a

Code: Select all

apt-get install alsa-utils
solved the problem. __{{emoticon}}__

Just as a hint for others.
