    Yes you can.

    This is what I have in my /etc/xorg.conf (I prefer Ctrl Shift)

    Section"InputDevice"
    Identifier"Keyboard0"
    Driver"keyboard"
    Option"CoreKeyboard"
    Option"XkbModel""pc105"
    Option"XkbLayout""us,el"
    Option"XKbOptions""grp:ctrl_shift_toggle"
    EndSection
