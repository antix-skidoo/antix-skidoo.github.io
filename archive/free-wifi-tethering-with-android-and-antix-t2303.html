I didn't have to change anything to tether my phone and use the wifi connection.

All I did was make the wired-default usb0 in wicd (if that's not it check in ceni). I can then connect to the local network using my phone, and just connect to the phone over usb (my antiX laptop is too old for bluetooth). I'm using Antix m11.
