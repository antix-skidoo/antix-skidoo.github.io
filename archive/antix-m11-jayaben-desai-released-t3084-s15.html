If you just want to use the usb stick to boot antiX try the isohybrid method:

dd if=name_of_the_iso.iso of=/dev/sd_name_of_the_flash_drive

eg

dd if=antiX-M11.iso of=/dev/sdc

you might need a rootdelay=20
