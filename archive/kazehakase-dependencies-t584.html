Firstly, welcome to antix.

I presume you are using antiX-M7 or antiX-M7.01

In that case, check that the Mepis repos are disabled from /etc/apt/sources.list or in synaptic. Once they are do an apt-get upgrade in a terminal or from synaptic reload and then try to install kazehakase.

The newest (presently testing version of antiX , antiX-M7.2-preview1) installs kazehakase with a simple apt-get install kazehakase.
