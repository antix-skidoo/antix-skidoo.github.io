There is no screensaver as such. What is installed is xlockmore. Choose your screensaver from the menu and this will lock the screen. To unlock, give user password.

If you want a 'normal' screensaver app, I suggest xscreensaver. It is bigger than xlockmore, but is still light compared to kde ones, and works as a normal screensaver and for locking the screen.
