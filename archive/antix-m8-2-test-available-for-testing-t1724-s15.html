Here's a preliminary report from a dial-up user.

I'm confirming what Mepis 8 users have been reporting on Mepislovers for a long time now: AntiX-M8.2-test exhibits none of the system-freeze-on-disconnect that has crippled Mepis 8 for a lot of dial-up users, making antiX the OS of choice for a lot of users lacking wireless or cable.

BTW, this test .iso is darn-near unbreakable. It took me four serial-sessions of downloading at the local library in order to get the whole file. But the whole thing burned fine and launches great. Video, audio, modem...all detected perfectly and running right off the bat.

Very nice overall design (pretty radical for me, since I'm jumping from antiX 7.5).
