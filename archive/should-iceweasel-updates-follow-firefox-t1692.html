there are 3 debians repos(not including the experimental one):
unstable: the programs have bugs and many packages get broken often
testing: the programs have minor bugs or none and the packages get broken rarely
stable: everything works and nothing gets broken to keep the system stable __{{emoticon}}__

every program passes through the debian repos from unstable > testing > stable. so for example iceweasel needs to stay some time in the unstable repos to find bugs or an incompatibility with other packages or dependencies, then it gets moved in testing and after a time it gets in stable. this is my understanding of the debian repos.
