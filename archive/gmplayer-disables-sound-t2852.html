This might help others diagnose a sound problem.

Playing a soundless DVD in gmplayer (old home movies) somehow muted the sound and then disabled sound system-wide. I couldn't diagnose why I'd lost sound until backtracking my actions led back to gmplayer. Unmuting sound in gmplayer restored sound throughout the system when I'd exhausted all other options.

I'm not sure why this happened or if it's a known issue. Couldn't find any posts on it. Does everyone but me know that muted sound in gmplayer disables sound when all other interfaces (volume control, mixer controls, flash controls online) show sound working fine? If not, maybe it's something to look at in the next antiX release.

Meanwhile, anyone with undiagnosed sound problems: Check the mute button in gmplayer!
