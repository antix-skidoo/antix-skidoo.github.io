antiXcontrolcentre->Edit icewm->Preferences

Change
WorkspaceNames=" 1"," 2"," 3"," 4" to

WorkspaceNames=" 1"," 2"

You'll need to restart icewm for it to take effect (In menu-logout-restart icewm)
