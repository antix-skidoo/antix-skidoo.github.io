Greetings, samnjugu! Welcome to the antiX forum. Glad you were able to install antiX; hope that everything works out the way you would like it. This is a very warm community, in terms of being friendly and being willing to share and offer advice, and antiX (in my biased opinion) makes an excellent base upon which to create whatever custom environment you like. In case you are not aware of it, there are two versions of antiX that are available, a full version, which includes IceWM and Fluxbox window managers and a light, but complete, assortment of applications, then there is a BASE version, which includes Fluxbox and only a few other core components, from which someone can build their own customized system. We also have tools that are mentioned in the forum and in the documentation about building your own respin or remaster, and there are several in the community who do just that. If any of those things interest you, poke around - it's all here.

Enjoy your stay - hope you decide to stay! __{{emoticon}}__
