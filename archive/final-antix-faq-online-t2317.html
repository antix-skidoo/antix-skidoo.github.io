Silverbear, it has been a while since I looked at the antiX documentation. You have done a great job in updating the documentation and including the new features, while in general keeping the document as straightforward as possible. Perhaps antiX is not, or should not be considered, a first time user's system. You have done a great job, however, making the system into something that anyone who has an inquisitive nature and the ability to read and follow instructions should have no trouble getting started with antiX, and of course, anti and his small (but excellent) team of helpers have done their usual great job of producing a reasonably easy, fast, and very flexible system that gives us a nice alternative to the simple and stable SimplyMEPIS.

I keep telling my friends who at least know how to install software to give antiX a try. I've gotten a few of them to come around. One in particular carries a USB instance of antiX with him when he travels, and he find it to be quite useful and helpful.

Thanks for all that everyone has done to make this the kind of system that I can recommend to others! I try to test and make suggestions from time to time so that we can reach these kinds of people, and to me, the results have been quite positive indeed!
