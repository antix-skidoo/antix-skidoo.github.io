    rokytnji wrote:I do not do windows , so I will back out so other members can reply.
    Just for future reference, to Connectshares it does not matter whether the shares are coming from a Windows box or a Linux box.

    Provided the share server system:
    1. Publishes the shares to the LAN
    2. Grants access rights on each published share, to the operator of the remote workstation
    Connectshares will mount them on the antiX workstation system.
