gnomic,

at menu, press the down key and the rundown timer will stop.

usbmount is used to automount usb devices and it is not working correctly. Even as live, remove usbmount, and mount your devices manually (until I find a fix) by using fdisk -l (in a root terminal to detect your device) and in a user terminal type pmount /dev/xxxx and it should mount in Rox under /media as user (providing the stick is owned by user and not root).
