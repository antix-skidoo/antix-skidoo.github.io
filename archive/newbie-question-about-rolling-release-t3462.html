new packages are coming to testing and unstable almost everyday. the old packages are replaced by the new ones, but the archives for the new packages remain in /var/cache/apt/archives. to remove them use apt-get clean. also if you want to save some disk space i would recommend removing unused locales by installing localepurge. sometime ago i posted what i do to save space on my install, have a look at it if you want. post18498.html#p18498
edit: also you can clear your browser cache, mine gets to about 300 mb weekly.
