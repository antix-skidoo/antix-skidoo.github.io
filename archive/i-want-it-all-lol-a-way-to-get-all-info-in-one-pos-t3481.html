First thing I just found out. Before upgradiing anything you need to
purge myspell-el-gr and to do that open terminal as root and paste
this in there: apt-get purge myspell-el-gr

Then paste this in there: apt-get update

Then paste this: apt-get dist-upgrade

If you do this first before you update your system you will not get
this error:
