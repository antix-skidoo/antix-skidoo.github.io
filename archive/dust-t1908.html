Hello. Dust desktop themes seem to be fairly popular right now. Since I've been wanting something a little different, I decided to try putting together a dust theme for icewm. I found a nice icon and gtk2 theme at gnome-look as well as a matching xmms skin, a wallpaper at customize.org, and an icewm theme at box-look. I liked it a lot; however, the window border was too glossy for me. So I looked at some screenshots and decided that most of the dust themes were fairly subdued. Then my brain made the connection: use the taskbar from the icewm dust theme and the windows from the icegil grey theme. Here's the result:


And here's the theme:


Remember to choose the extract to option and extract it to ~/.icewm/themes in order to keep the symlinks intact.

I'm going to work on a second version of this theme that has an application icon in the top-left corner of the window as you'd see in gnome.

Enjoy (or not).
