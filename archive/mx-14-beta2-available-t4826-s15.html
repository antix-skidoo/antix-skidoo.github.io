You're right, its non-obvious!

Right click on panel, select panel>panel preferences.

In the dialog box that opens, in Mode select Horizontal from the drop down list. It will probably put your taskbar to the top.

Now uncheck"lock taskbar" and a handle will appear on the taskbar. Drag at the handle to where ever you want the taskbar.
