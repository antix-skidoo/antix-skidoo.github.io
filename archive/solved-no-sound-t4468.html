rokytnji wrote:

Code: Select all

   speaker-test -Dplug:front -c2
There's an inbuilt test wav file. Try this, it plays the wav, cycles left and right front channels, and loops through three times. It also sounds better than white/pink noise. It even tells you whether your speakers are connected the right way round.

Code: Select all

speaker-test -c 2 -t wav -l 3
Alternatively

Code: Select all

speaker-test --channels 2 --test wav --nloops 3

Edit
Added command using long form of switches
