Just as info; I have installed antiX-12-base-rc2 to an 8gb sdhc card, & am running my Novatech N1 netbook from it.

(I'm loving this little distro - thanks guys)


Edit: Forgot to mention, I did have to alter boot/grub/menu.lst & etc/fstab on the first boot, as the drives came up wrong.
