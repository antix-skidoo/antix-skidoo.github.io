Put this in

AntiX Control Center>Edit Icewm Settings > toolbar next to removable devices

prog"Unplug Removable Device" /usr/share/icons/Faenza-Cupertino-mini/devices/32/drive-removable-media-usb.png unplugdrive.sh
prog"Quit" /usr/share/icons/Faenza-Cupertino-mini/actions/32/application-exit.png desktop-session-exit

Save. Close Geany.

On toolbar

Menu > Logout > retart icewm

You should now have a red shutdown button in your toolbar. Left click on it. There is now your icon,

Thanks,
I right click the desktop and get:
terminal
Terminal is all you need. Type in

Code: Select all

sudo shutdown -h now
or

Code: Select all

sudo shutdown -h 0
and it should shut down BUT shutting down is not what you want to be doing, is it? You want to change to Icewm Desktop from Fluxbox, which you are in, so:

Right click in the window and a menu appears, as you have already found out. Now left click on Desktop/Other Desktops/Icewm and it should change over giving you your usual Desktop. then you can start and shutdown in your usual way.
