The empty trash or recycle bin option in pcmanfm doesn't seem to work. But as secipolla suggested, you can browse to ~/.local/share/trash and delete them. I've sometimes had to have root permission to do this.

As with Windows, if you are deleting something in pcmanfm, you can hold the shift key down, and the files will just delete and not go to the trash bin.
