The meta-installer is for after antiX has been installed to hard-disk (well, not quite as you can use it in livecd mode)
The multimedia-phonetics option is not what you want.
You need the Non-free video to get the necessary codecs, libdvdcss2 and lame to play encrypted dvd and mp3.
You then need to install a video and audio player. You probably want mplayer-nogui (it also plays audio files)
