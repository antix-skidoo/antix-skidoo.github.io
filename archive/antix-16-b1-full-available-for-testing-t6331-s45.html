dolphin_oracle wrote:looking at conky, samK's wireless at eth0 should show up in conky once connected. but his wired network (eth1) never will.
The fix is to include the following between the eth0 and wlan0 sections in ~/.conkyrc.

Code: Select all

${if_up eth1}${color}eth1 up: $alignr${color3} ${upspeed eth1}
${color}$alignr${upspeedgraph   eth1 30,170 5599cc 5599cc}
${color}eth1 down: $alignr${color3} ${downspeed eth1}
${color2}$alignr${downspeedgraph eth1 30,170  5599cc 5599cc}${endif}
