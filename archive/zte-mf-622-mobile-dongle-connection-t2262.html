This must be one of those modems that needs switching. Well, then you'll have probably to use usbmodeswitch. I'm am not in my machine now, so I can't explain much further. But search google for your model and usbmodeswitch instructions.

Switching means that linux thinks your modem is a removable media, and the removable media (usb-storage) has to be dettached and the modem attached.

After that you can connect using umtsmon, wvdial or any ppp dialer.
