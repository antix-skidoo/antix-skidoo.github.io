In case we're keeping a list somewhere of minimalist apps, either for users of antiX or for inclusion in later versions:

I've added a new word processor to my stable of writing tools: Wordgrinder. It's super light for the terminal, a tiny download, lightning fast, and only needs liblua5.1-0 and liblua5.1-filesystem0 (in the repos). It was developed for writers of fiction who want minimal distractions from the writing process. But it allows essential text formatting (underline, italics, block-indenting, bullet points), autosave, and exports to text or html.

Amazing.
