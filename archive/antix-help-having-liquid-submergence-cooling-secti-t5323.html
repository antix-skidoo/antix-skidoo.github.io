The basic thing to do when taking on an old computer is to remove any built up dust in it. Stick the vacuum cleaner near it as you blow or lightly brush the dusty areas. It is often the dust that makes the components overheat.
(As for immersing in oil, not me.)
However there are some liquid cooling systems available if you look around, if you have a mind to try.
