I'm aware that when people are writing about a country (say Ukraine, but also China, Russia, Israel, Iran, ...) they are actually discussing USA. This is always a problem, because it is reductionism in its worst form. I understand that not everyone can have a strong education in geography and regional history. I can give a tip how to recognize a bad article about foreign countries using two features:
- names of the countries are used as grammatical subjects and not as territories or locations,
- (last) names of country leaders are used synonymously with country names.
