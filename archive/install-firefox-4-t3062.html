right click on tar.bz2 in pacmanfm or left click in roxfm.

extract to /home (that is the way I run it on this unit)




My launchers for icewm (menu and toolbar) and fluxbox (menu only)
Icewm:

Code: Select all

prog"Firefox4" /home/harry/firefox/chrome/icons/default/default32.png /home/harry/firefox/firefox
Fluxbox:

Code: Select all

[exec] (Firefox4) {/home/harry/firefox/firefox}
Under Menu>Help>AntiX Faq you'll find how to edit Icewm menus and Fluxbox menus.
