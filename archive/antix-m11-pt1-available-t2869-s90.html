I've been pretty quiet during the testing phase. Actually, that has been a good thing. Everything I have tried in this release has worked great. I've used FULL and CORE in my testing, and actually had an extended session with both over the weekend. If there are issues, they are not crossing any of the areas that I have been exercising. Those I've been able to talk into trying antiX have, without exception, been favorably impressed. The only people I seem to have difficulty convincing to try antiX are those who are averse to trying new things. Having IceWM as our default instead of Fluxbox (even though many of the veterans really like Fluxbox) has been a good move. It seems to make things easier for the relative newcomers, and the veterans don't have any problem shifting to Fluxbox (or even reordering things so that Fluxbox comes up as their first choice).

I may explicitly try a few more of the images to see if I've missed something, but as I say, everything I have tried has been superb. I am not at all surprised. BTW, I've been going with the Liquorix kernel, but on the MEPIS side of the equation, I've not had the problems with Warren's kernels that others have been encountering. IF I find anything in my last minute hunts, I'll let you know, but I think we have things pretty well in hand here. Keep up the GREAT work!
