Hi guys. Installed MX15 on a machine I just built. Installed all the Nazi codecs, adjusted ALSA and Pulse as needed. Had Youtube going fine and went to Clementine. No sound and no sound in Skype. Screwed around a little and decided to dig around in the BIOS.
Gigabyte board.
Disabled local sound.
Waallah !
What happens is the HDMI setup seems to override"system" and takes care of the audio when the local was disabled(was set to auto) in the BIOS.
Dunno if it works outside of Gigabyte boards.
If sound is screwing with you, go look in your BIOS.

My guess is either way, disable and use your softwarez to choose and adjust sound.
