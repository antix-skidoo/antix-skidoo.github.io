Antix 13.2 Full is not a rolling release by default. It is set to Debian Wheezy/stable repos for maximun stability (as requested by some of our users).

Re synaptic - yes that is how most users of antiX would keep it up to date. By up to date I mean any new fixes will be updated not any latest versions of apps. (The price you pay for using stable.)

In Debian the terms mean basically this.

stable = unchanging. Only security, bug fix updates.
