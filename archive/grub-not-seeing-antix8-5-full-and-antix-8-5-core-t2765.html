change the last 2 entries to read:

title antiX-core
root (hd0,2)
kernel /boot/vmlinuz-2.6.32-1-mepis-smp root=/dev/hda3 ro nosplash quiet
initrd /boot/initrd.img-2.6.32-1-mepis-smp

title antiX-core (init-3)
root (hd0,2)
kernel /boot/vmlinuz-2.6.32-1-mepis-smp root=/dev/hda3 ro nosplash quiet 3
initrd /boot/initrd.img-2.6.32-1-mepis-smp


Or find out the uuid for /devhda3 using ls -l /dev/disk/by-uuid
