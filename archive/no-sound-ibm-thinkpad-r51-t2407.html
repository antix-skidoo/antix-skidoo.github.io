If me. I would add

Code: Select all

snd-intel8x0= Intel ICH (i8x0) chipsets
to the end of the file in /etc/modprobe.d/alsa-base.conf and reboot.

The reason I suggest this Anti is that alsa version does not show under his inxi sound card readout. Usually a Alsa Version will show up along with a driver being used in the sound card readout.
