Ok,

Open control center, then on the open tab named Desktop (should be the default for control center), click on the Edit IceWm Settings,
then you should see Geany open and a whole row of tabs click on the one named keys.
Then you will see the list of already installed keyboard shortcuts and the syntax to setup your own.
