So I ran the base version from iso and installed in a 11GB partition (a lot of room to grow and play).
First it didn't find the MEPIS file system, but I had been mean with it and had put the iso in an ext4 partiton. Reformatted to ext3, put lang=br and toram as boot codes and it loaded super fast. Then I booted directly to init 3, called smxi, updated (set the multimedia repos to stable just in case I choose to install mplayer), cleaned kernel modules, xorg modules, uninstalled bluetooth, wireless and such and installed 2.6.31-6 liquorix kernel. Rebooted (just in case) and installed nVidia latest and greatest.

The desktop is looking very nice, anti. Excellent option that exoodles (multimedia and other stuff installer) script (is it from h2 too?).
