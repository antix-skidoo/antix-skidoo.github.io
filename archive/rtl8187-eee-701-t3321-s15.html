I had the very same problem on a old IBM 390E that was dropped and even broke the left hinge on LCD. I fixed it and sold that Laptop with AntiX 8 on it. The problem was with the connector on the back of LCD screen ribbon cable had jarred loose when it dropped. Slapping both sides on the LCD screen plastic housing would restore the screen. I took the screen apart from screen housing. Pried it forward and reseated ribbon cable. I then secured ribbon cable to back of LCD screen with duct tape. I fixed the broken hinge with jb weld.
