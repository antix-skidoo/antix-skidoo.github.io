I decided to make antiX-core squeeze for those who wish to start off with a core system and then build up with stability as number one priority. Of course, changing the repos to testing or unstable should also work as well.
Users should be able to enable MEPIS repos and MEPIS CR repos and turn antiX-core into a lighter MEPIS kde if they wish.
(I think you could even use the Debian MINT repos, but I haven't tested that). or simply just keep to Debian.

All packages are up-to-date in squeeze as from 31 March.
