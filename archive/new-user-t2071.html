Hello
A new Antix user here. I have been happily using Mepis for about the past 2 years and like it very much. I have also experimented with Ubuntu, Zenwalk, Debian, Linux Mint, Knoppix, DSL, and Puppy Linux. I have always come back to Mepis as it just seems to be the best overall that I have used. With a recent hard drive failure on our primary PC, I dug an old unused desktop out of the closet to use for the time being. It is about 11 years old, HP with 500 MHz Celeron, 256 RAM, and 10 gig hard drive. After reading about Antix I thought it might be a nice fit for this computer (at least until I get a replacement hard drive for the other PC). I'm not a Linux expert by any means but I felt pretty comfortable getting things done in Mepis. I like this Antix so far but it is enough different that I am having to learn some new things again. I've read over the forums some but any advice for a brand new user is appreciated. Everything looks very nice. A major speed killer for me seems to be Iceweasel. I have tried Dillo and wasn't immediately impressed so I installed Midori. It seems much better. Very nice distro so far.
