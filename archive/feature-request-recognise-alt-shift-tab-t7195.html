In IceWM and Fluxbox the key binding alt+shift+tab already works as desired (in IceWM within the window-switcher-popup skidoo mentioned â€“ just repeatedly press and let go the tab key while keeping the other two keys, alt and shift, pressed).

For JWM insert the line

Code: Select all

<Key mask="AS" key="Tab">prev</Key>
into the file ~/.jwm/keys and restart JWM.
