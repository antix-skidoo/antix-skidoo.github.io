    I myself have noticed that if the printer is NOT connected during installation, the printer will default to PDF Print. Once the printer is installed, the default DOES NOT change (it stays PDF Printer). Use Synaptic to install"hplip" and associated programs. Running"hp-setup" should solve the driver problem for the HP printer. But be aware that the HP printer may not be installed as DEFAULT.
