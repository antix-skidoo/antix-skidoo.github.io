If you look in the theme file you're using , under the menu entry , just pick a larger font.
<MenuStyle>
<Font>Terminus Bold-14</Font>
<Foreground>black</Foreground>
<Background>#DEB887</Background>
<ActiveForeground>white</ActiveForeground>
<ActiveBackground>#DEB887:#6F4E37</ActiveBackground>
</MenuStyle>
In this example the font is pretty big at 14 , most are 12 that you normally see.
This makes the fly-out menu taller also to match the larger font size.
I like larger font sizes , as my eyes are not getting any younger.
Experiment with all the font sizes , it really changes the overall look of the desktop.
Hope this helps.
