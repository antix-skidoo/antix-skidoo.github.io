$ nano .xinitrc

this is what I got in mine:

Code: Select all

#!/bin/sh
setxkbmap -option terminate:ctrl_alt_bksp
xsetroot -solid black
unclutter -idle 2 &
numlockx &

exec icewm
save/exit/reboot to test it.
